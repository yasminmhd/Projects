Grooming

Because every pet deserves to look paw-some!

What's Included:

  • Bath & Blow Dry:
    A gentle cleansing bath using pet-safe shampoos, followed by a full blow dry to keep your pet fresh, fluffy, and smelling great.
  • Haircuts & Styling:
    Breed-specific or custom trims tailored to your pet’s needs and your preferences—whether it’s a full makeover or a simple touch-up.
  • Nail Trimming:
    Regular nail care prevents discomfort, injuries, and posture problems. Our groomers ensure a stress-free experience for your furry friend.
  • Ear Cleaning:
    Essential for preventing infections and buildup. We gently clean the ears using vet-approved products to maintain ear health and hygiene.
