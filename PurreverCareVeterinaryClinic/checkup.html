General Checkup

Keeping your furry friend in tip-top shape!

What's Included:

  • Full-body examination:
    A nose-to-tail physical check to assess your pet’s overall condition, including weight, posture, joints, abdomen, and general demeanor.
  • Heart and lung check:
    We listen to your pet’s heart for murmurs or irregular rhythms and check lung function for any signs of respiratory issues or infections.
  • Skin, fur, and dental assessment:
    We examine your pet’s coat for signs of dryness, parasites, or infections, inspect the skin for lumps or irritations, and perform a dental checkup to assess gum health and tartar buildup.
